How RandomLibs Works!
Random Libs takes Mad Libs to a more nonsensical approach. First you fill out the fields with whatever adjective, place, verb, or person that you want. After clicking submit, you will receive a random story with your fields randomly filled in. You may get a noun where a verb was supposedto be or vise versa. Now you can have hilarity with stories that make even less sense than their mad libs counterparts!
